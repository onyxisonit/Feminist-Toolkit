What belongs in your feminist tool kit? How do you envision engaging this resource as you move forward, living your own feminist life?

When decontructing what being a feminist means to me, I thought of all of my lived experiences. After pondering over everything I have gone through, I delegated my childhood as the most imperative influence to my feminist journey. I was exposed to sex at a very young age. As a result, my feminist journey has largely been trying to unshackle myself from the belief that my sexual being is inheretly tied to pleasing others, to figure out what it means to be a sexual being for myself.
